The Real Mojito

This Cuban cocktail is the perfect balance of sweet and tart, while still being very very refreshing during the scorching-hot weather. The use of fresh mint and lime help it accomplish its goal of "refreshing" while the rum gives it that kick we all need during the summer.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 ounces of White Rum
  • Fresh Mint Leaves
  • 3 Lime Wedges
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • Ice
  • Half cup Club Soda

Steps

  1. Place mint leaves and 1 lime wedge in a sturdy glass and crush to release oils
  2. Add remaining wedges with the sugar and crush again
  3. Fill glass with ice and pour the rum and club soda
  4. Stir and enjoy!
