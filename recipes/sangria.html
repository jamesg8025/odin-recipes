Sangria! Sangria!

Description

Sangria is an alcoholic fruit punch that originates from Portugal and Spain. While there is also a white variation of the drink, this recipe is for the more-common red variation along with the various fruits that complement it in the summer heat.

Ingredients

  • Red Wine
  • Brandy
  • Triple Sec
  • Orange Juice
  • Lemon Juice
  • Frozen Lemonade Concentrate
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Carbonated Water (optional)
  • Sliced Lemon
  • Sliced Orange
  • Sliced Lime
  • Maraschino Cherries

Steps

  1. Mix all the liquid ingredients along with the sugar in a bowl
  2. Add the slices of orange, lemon, and lime and the maraschino cherries
  3. Serve immediately over ice in a pitcher and refrigerate overnight
  4. To add some fizz to the sangria, add carbonated water just before serving
  5. Stir and enjoy!
