The Salty Chihuahua

Description

The Salty Chihuahua is a variation of the famous Salty Dog. While the latter makes use of juicy grapefruit, the former uses tart lime juice and natural lemonade!

Ingredients

  • 1 Lime Wedge
  • Lime Juice
  • Coarse Salt
  • Ice
  • 1 ounce of Jigger Tequila
  • 5 ounces of lemonade

Steps

  1. Wet the rim of an old fashioned glass with lime juice
  2. Dip the rim in salt
  3. Fill glass with ice
  4. Pour tequila and lemonade
  5. Squeeze and drop lime wedge
  6. Stir and enjoy!
